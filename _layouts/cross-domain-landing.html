Path: https://schema.bra0.org/cross-domain/

Hand-written parent landing per ADR-058 §2.11. The cross-domain/ path itself has no ontology of its own — it groups mid-level vocabularies hosted under schema.bra0.org/cross-domain/<name>#.

Scope

Cross-domain ontologies sit between the foundational upper layer (BFO 2020) and the bra0 Knowledge-Space domain layer. They capture domain-agnostic patterns reused across multiple bra0 vocabularies (capability, evidence-os) and demos.
